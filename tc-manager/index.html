How to use the Team Captain Manager

A short guide for team captains — everything you need for your own team.

You have received a ready-made contact list for your team. Nothing needs to be set up — just open the tool and start contacting the brothers on your team.
1
Fill in your details (once)

In Captain setup at the top, enter your name and paste your WhatsApp group link. These two entries are automatically inserted into the welcome message, so you don't have to type them each time.

2
Pick your team

Use the coloured tabs — Lobby, Cafeteria, Auditorium. The number on each tab shows how many people are in it. All shows everybody. A tag such as Welcome on a row means that person also serves in another department; a ⚑ 2 teams mark means they are assigned to more than one team — worth keeping in mind when you plan.

3
Get in touch

There are two green/teal WhatsApp buttons and they do different things:

  • Green, with the phone number — opens a plain WhatsApp chat, no text filled in. For a quick personal message.
  • Teal “Chat + text” — opens WhatsApp with your complete welcome message already written. Just check it and send.

Copy copies the mobile number, Copy text copies the welcome message to the clipboard (handy for email or SMS), and QR contact shows a QR code — hold your phone camera over it and the phone offers to save the person straight into your contacts. Your captain notes are never part of the code.

4
Adjust the welcome message

The template under Captain setup can be edited freely. The placeholders {firstName} {team} {chatLink} {captainName} are replaced automatically for each person. Reset to default restores the original wording.

5
Your own notes

The Captain notes column is yours — e.g. “called, cannot attend Saturday”. Notes are saved automatically as you type.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays in this browser on this device only — nothing is uploaded anywhere. That also means: clearing your browser data deletes the list. Use Export JSON now and then to keep a backup file, and don't use private/incognito mode.

What is the “MergeMaster” tab?

MergeMaster is a separate collecting pool and has nothing to do with your team list — it is used by the brother who prepares the lists. Contact details for the assemblies and the regional convention are kept in two other tools (the Assembly and RC organisation charts). MergeMaster gathers people from both, combines duplicate entries, and lets him tick exactly who is needed for the next event and export that selection as a ready-to-use list.

Adding someone to the pool never changes your team — your list stays exactly as you received it. You can simply leave this tab alone.

Other buttons you normally won't need

Import & merge and Find duplicates are also for whoever prepares the lists — your team list is already loaded. Reset deletes everything, so please only use it if you're asked to.

Possible duplicates

Choose which record to keep. Empty fields are filled from the others; teams are combined.
